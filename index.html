About Computer Science

Computer science is the study of computers. It includes their theory, design, development, and application. It is a broad and dynamic field, from software and hardware. Things like algorithms, data analysis, and artificial intelligence are subfields of it. Computer scientists use mathematical principles and logical thinking to problems and create new technologies.

About AP CSP

AP Computer Science Principles is an introductory course to the field of computer science. It covers a wide array of topics, including algorithms, the internet, cybersecurity, and how our society is impacted by computers. It is aimed so people develop computer related skills. Further courses on computer science are available for more exploration on the topic at CCA. Mr. Hare is currently teaching this course at CCA.
